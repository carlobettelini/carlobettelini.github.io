About me:

My name is Carlo Bettelini. I was born in 1994, I am 1,9 meters (74,8 inch), so that makes me pretty tall. I am very creative, and I like making short films in my spare time, as well as some video games. Those who know me well know that I am thoughtful, collaborative, and determined.

Since when I was a kid I started getting invested in creating short videos, and in primary school I produced my first serious works. When I was in highscool in 2013 I won the second place at Tagliacorto with Internalization.

CISA

After that I managed to enter CISA, a training institute in the field of cinema, television and multimedia culture. I had many chances to develop short videos, and even after I finished it, I continued to collaborate with them in many occasions. You can see most of my works I did after that period in the videomaker section.

USI
