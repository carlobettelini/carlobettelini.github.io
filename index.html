Oltre il Confine - Trailer

2016, Switzerland, 12 min, Daniele Lucca

Three Italians with money problems try to do a robbery in Switzerland, in a petrol station where one of the three worked before being fired. They just glean a handful of francs. They are disappointed but feel hunted. The night on the run will lead them to discover more about themselves and it will bring them face to face with one of the most important decisions of their life: to cross the border or not.
