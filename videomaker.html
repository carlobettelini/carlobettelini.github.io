Here is a partial list of all works I did as a videomaker:

RETE UNO

Gustando

I was hired to realize video summaries of Fabrizio Casati's radio program Gustando, a food and wine journey around the most succulent events in Switzerland, and I was also a tutor for CISA students, who followed me to gain experience in the field. The purpose was to created something creative and original, while also make people better aware of local productions. Initially a shorter version of the videos was also broadcasted on television weekly on the program "Filo diretto". A very beautiful and funny experience.

Uno... che pedala

For two weeks I followed Julien Carton in his radio program "Uno... che pedala", which purpose was to interview guests while carrying them on a funny blue rickshaw through the beautiful lands of Switzerland. It was a very funny experience!
